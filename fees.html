A) Enrollment & One-Off Fees

Paid once at registration (or annually where applicable).

  • Registration: €50 (new players)
  • Uniform kit: €75 (2 kits)
  • Insurance: €30 (annual)

Families joining mid-season may receive pro-rated fees depending on start date.

B) Monthly Training Fees

Includes training sessions, match preparation, and coaching support.

  • U10–U13: €40 / month
  • U14–U17: €50 / month
  • Elite Program: €80 / month

Payment due by the 5th of each month. Payment plans available on request.

C) Scholarships & Support

We support talented players and families facing financial difficulty.

  • Need-based: decision within 7–14 days
  • Performance-based: monthly review
  • Sponsored slots: as available

D) FAQs

Can I pay weekly? Monthly is preferred, but we can arrange a plan.

Siblings discount? Available where possible depending on category and situation.

Missed payment? We’ll contact you privately to find a solution.

Uniform mandatory? Yes for official training and matches; support is available if needed.
