Admissions & Enrolment

Join Global Sport Academy and access elite football training alongside education and life-skills development.

Enrol Now View Programs Contact Us

Who Can Apply

Boys and girls aged 6–19 for football programs.
Students aged 16+ for education and sport science courses.
Scholarships available for outstanding talents.

Admissions Process

Step 1: Submit enrolment or trial request form.
Step 2: Screening and assessment.
Step 3: Trial session or interview.
Step 4: Confirmation and onboarding.

Important Information

All applicants must provide accurate personal and medical information. Parental consent is required for minors.
